Flexible Duct Hose

A flexible duct hose is a versatile, bendable tube designed for ventilation, heating, cooling, and exhaust systems. They are valued for their flexibility, ease of installation, and ability to navigate tight spaces and complex layouts. Commonly used in HVAC systems, industrial ventilation, and automotive applications.
